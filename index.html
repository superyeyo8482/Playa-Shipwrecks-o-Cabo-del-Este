⚖️ Legal Certainty for Foreigners (Fideicomiso)

In Mexico’s “restricted zone” (50 km from coast), foreigners acquire property through a Fideicomiso – a bank trust that guarantees full ownership rights.

  • You are the beneficiary – full rights to use, sell, rent, or inherit
  • Bank holds legal title as trustee (fiduciary)
  • 50‑year renewable term (can be renewed indefinitely)
  • For co‑investment: two beneficiaries can be named (50/50)
